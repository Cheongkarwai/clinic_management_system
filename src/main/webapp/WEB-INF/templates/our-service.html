Free Checkups

A routine checkup is a visit to a primary care physician with the goal of keeping one's health in check. Routine checks are more concerned with avoiding sickness than with treating a current symptom or a previously diagnosed condition.

Expert Doctors

Physicians are medical experts since the word physic originally applied to both the profession of medicine and natural science.

Vaccination

Vaccination is the process of administering a vaccine to aid the immune system in developing immunity to a disease. Vaccines comprise a weakened, live, or deceased microbe or virus, as well as proteins or toxins from the organism. They help prevent illness from infectious diseases by increasing the body's adaptive immunity.

Medicines

Medicines are chemicals or substances that are used to treat, stop, or prevent disease, alleviate symptoms, or aid in the identification of ailments. Medical advancements have enabled doctors to heal numerous ailments and save lives.

Bed Facility

Regularly maintained and manned, and always ready to care for admitted patients. They cover beds for patients who are formally admitted to a facility for treatment and care and stay for at least one night.

Total Care

Total care refers to long-term care institutions that are responsible for addressing all of a resident's requirements. While some patients receiving so-called complete care may be able to satisfy all or some of their needs for daily living activities without the aid of a care giver, the facility and its personnel must supervise the resident to ensure those needs are satisfied.
